Why Minouche Layouts?

The idea to make layout components is not really new. Uiuxassets.com brought in june 2016 a free asset library by Johan Netzler (@JohanNetzler), a Swedish/French designer and founder of Basic Atom.

This was the point we decided to create Minouche. Not just the templates are really cool and as Sketch assets available. The fact to use the new Foundation Grid 6 was enough to help and develop templates faster and comprehensible.

We used for Minouche: Ux-Flowchart-Cards from Uiuxassets. For the grids mostly Foundation Flex Grid 6.2.3 for sites. As fonts we used Montserrat by Google Fonts and Redacted Regular made by Christian Naths (@christiannaths).

Further we used object-fit and some masonry techniques, we learned by the way to create fast results.
